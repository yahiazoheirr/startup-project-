1. Startup presentation

  1. This app helps users plan personalized itineraries using an interactive chatbot and filters. After logging in, users choose their budget, availability, activity type (tourism or leisure), and food preferences. The chatbot generates several itinerary options that can be discussed and modified in real time. Users can save, favorite, and customize their itineraries. The goal is to simplify daily trip planning while adapting to each user’s preferences and constraints.
